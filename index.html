Frequently asked questions

Do I need an internet connection for Find My Pet to work?

Yes, you need to have an internet connection on your phone in order for the app to work.

What is the range over which the tracker works?

Good news! Find My Pet has no distance limitation.

Can I use the Find My Pet for my rabbit?

Find My Pet can be used for cats, dogs, and other animals. As long as they can be chipped Find My Pet will work just fine.

Will I be able to see my pet moving in real-time?

Yes, you will see your pet moving in real time.

Can I use Find My Pet in my country?

Yes, Find My Pet works in every country.

How many pets can I track with Find My Pet?

As many as you want.
